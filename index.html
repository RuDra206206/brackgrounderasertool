How to Use the Background Eraser

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking to browse
  2. Select colors to erase by clicking "Add Color" or using the color picker
  3. Adjust the tolerance to control how similar colors are affected
  4. Use edge feathering to soften the edges of your subject
  5. Click "Erase Background" to process the image
  6. Download your image in your preferred format

Tips for Best Results

  • For complex backgrounds, add multiple colors to erase
  • Use higher tolerance for gradient backgrounds
  • Lower feathering values work best for sharp edges
  • PNG format preserves transparency, while JPEG/WebP offer smaller file sizes
